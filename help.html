Assignment 1 Peer Marking

The peer marking page allows you to rate the work of the other students in COMP249 for Assignment 1. The final ratings given will be used as part of the final score for the assignment.

Before you start you must enter your Student ID and your name you must have submitted the assignment to rate someone elses work so your student ID is checked against the list of submitted work. Of course, if you know someone elses student ID you could vote for them, but that wouldn't be nice. If someone has voted for you, let me know and I'll clear the votes.

You can only connect once to this application with a given Student ID. So, please don't provide your ID if you don't have time to rate at least 10 stylesheets (10 minutes?). You will only be allowed to rate 20 stylesheets at most.

You are asked to rate the stylesheet on two dimensions:

  • Visual Appearance - is the overall effect of the stylesheet attractive and does it make the play easy and pleasurable to read?
  • Use of Technology - does the stylesheet make good use of CSS features rather than just changing the font and colour of various elements. (Wow, how did they do that == Good)

You are also asked to provide some feedback to the student. Use this to make a comment if you want to say more than the scales allow. Try to imagine what you'd like to hear about your own work. Please don't insult anyone; I reserve the right to filter the comments if needed.

Please give some time to considering your rating.

Cheating If it is obvious that this process has been abused then I won't use the results in marking the assignment. Abuse could be the use of an automated script to vote for stylesheets, attempts to vote for your own script, etc. Please respect this process and rate the work of your peers fairly.
